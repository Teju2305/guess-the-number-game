🎯 Guess the Number: Quick Rules 🎯

  1. Objective: Guess the number between 1 and 100.
  2. Chances: You have 5 attempts to find the right number.
  3. How to Play: Enter your guess and click “Check”.
  4. Hints:
    • Way off 😣 — Very far.
    • Still far 😐 — Getting closer.
    • Getting closer 😊 — Almost there!
    • Perfect Answer 🎉 — You got it!
  5. Out of Attempts? The correct number will be shown.
  6. Reset: Click “Reset” to start over.

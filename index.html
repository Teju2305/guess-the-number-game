Can You Guess the Right Number?

Put your guessing skills to the test in this fun and challenging number guessing game! The objective is simple: guess the hidden number correctly within a limited number of attempts.

How to Play:

  1. Make Your Guess: Enter a number and submit your guess.
  2. Get Feedback: The game will tell you if your guess is too high, too low, or just right!
  3. Adjust Your Guesses: Use the feedback to narrow down the possibilities and guess again.
  4. Win or Lose: Guess the number before you run out of attempts to win. If you use all your attempts, the game will reveal the hidden number.

Game Tips:

  • Start with the Middle: Begin with a number in the middle of the range to maximize your chances of narrowing down the correct number quickly.
  • Use Feedback Wisely: If the game says your guess is far off, adjust your next guess significantly.
